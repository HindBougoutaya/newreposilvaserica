Our Quantifiable Goals

  • 500 households remain engaged and attend meetings each weekend
  • Plant 500 mulberry trees with 85% survival rate
  • 75% of households generate their first income from silk
  • 70% of households report better mental health and well-being
  • 75% of households participating in knowledge sharing
  • 12 sessions of literacy support conducted by adults

The Silk Process

1

Mulberry Cultivation

Families plant and nurture mulberry trees, creating sustainable food sources for silkworms while improving air quality.

2

Silkworm Care

Daily feeding and care of silkworms, teaching patience, responsibility, and unconditional love for animals.

3

Silk Harvesting

Carefully harvesting silk threads while maintaining ethical practices and respect for nature's cycles.

4

Weaving & Design

Transforming raw silk into beautiful Victorian-inspired garments with modern styling and elegance.
