Adopt Your Silkworm Family

Join the Silk Revolution - Every Family Should Adopt Silkworms!

Transform your home into a silk-producing haven and reconnect with nature's most luxurious fiber. By adopting silkworms, you're not just raising insects - you're nurturing a legacy of sustainability, elegance, and self-sufficiency.

ü•ö

Step 1: Choose Your Silkworm Family

Select from our curated Bombyx mori silkworm eggs. Each family contains 50-100 eggs, perfect for household production.

  • Premium quality eggs from healthy moths
  • 95% hatch rate guarantee
  • Detailed incubation instructions included
üåø

Step 2: Prepare Your Mulberry Garden

Plant mulberry trees to ensure a steady food supply. Each silkworm consumes about 2kg of fresh leaves during its lifecycle.

  • Plant 2-3 mulberry trees per 50 silkworms
  • Trees mature in 2-3 years
  • Leaves can be frozen for winter feeding
üè†

Step 3: Set Up Your Silkworm Habitat

Create the perfect environment with our starter kit or DIY setup using common household items.

  • Ventilated containers with paper lining
  • Temperature: 77-86¬∞F (25-30¬∞C)
  • Humidity: 65-85%
  • Clean, quiet space away from direct sunlight
üçÉ

Step 4: Daily Care & Feeding

Nurture your silkworms with fresh mulberry leaves twice daily. Watch them grow and transform before your eyes!

  • Feed young worms with tender, chopped leaves
  • Provide whole leaves for mature silkworms
  • Clean habitat daily to prevent disease
  • Handle gently with soft brushes
üßµ

Step 5: Harvest Your Golden Silk

After 25-30 days, your silkworms will spin cocoons. Harvest the silk and begin your journey into textile creation!

  • Each cocoon contains 300-900 meters of silk
  • Harvest before moths emerge for continuous thread
  • Learn reeling, spinning, and weaving techniques
